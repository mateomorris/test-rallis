About

— Foteini Chalkia

CHALKIA is a premium fashion brand that creates statement pieces for special occasions made of quality sustainable materials and inspired by one-of-a-kind human stories which offer a unique experience to you.

Foteini Chalkia invites you to her conceptual world. Her designs are known for their innovative concepts and unique aesthetic. She explores the possibilities of outfits for special occasions with the bridal and haute coutoure collections.

She is inspired by silhouettes, fabrics, sounds and stories.

The modern fearless woman is her muse.
